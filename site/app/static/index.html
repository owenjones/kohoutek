Kohoutek 2021

Kohoutek is (normally) a competition and activity day for Scouts in Avon County, and Guides in Bristol and South Gloucestershire, and Somerset North counties, organised by students from the University of Bristol Guides and Scouts society (UOBGAS).

Due to the uncertainty over the coming months around being able to run large events in person, for 2021 we're planning to run Kohoutek as an online event.

How will it work?

This year's Under the Sea themed competition will run throughout March 2021, with groups being provided activities to attempt during one of their regular section meetings, either indoors, outdoors, or virtually.

Groups should pick five activites from the list, and in teams complete them as best they can, before submitting their scores online.

We'll add up the scores and publish them in April. While the main Kohoutek trophies are only open to teams who would normally be able to enter the competition, there'll be a special Kohoutek 2021 trophy that all teams can compete for.

Rules

This is a competition after all! For this online event we've relaxed most of the normal rules, and have opened up the competition to Scouts and Guides from outside of the area.

Badges?

Yes, there'll be badges! You can order these, as well as badges from the 2019 and 2020 events, once you've signed up your group.

Sign up for Kohoutek 2021

For 2021, Kohoutek is open to any Scout Troops or Guide Units that wish to enter, with no limit to the number of teams you can have.

Only Scouts in Avon County, and Guides in Bristol and South Gloucestershire or Somerset North Counties, will be eligable to compete for the Kohoutek trophies however.
